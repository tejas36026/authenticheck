AuthentiCheck: Deep Dive into Originality

Our multi-layered analysis goes beyond surface scans to reveal the true genesis of student work.

Multi-Vector Plagiarism Search

Cross-references text against billions of web pages, academic journals, and an internal repository of past submissions.

Advanced AI Authorship Detection

Utilizes sophisticated linguistic analysis to identify patterns indicative of AI-generated content.

Writing Pattern Forensics

Analyzes typing rhythm (if data available), paste activity, editing velocity, and style consistency for authorship cues.

Granular Originality Reports

Provides clear, actionable reports highlighting suspicious sections, source matches, AI probability, and writing anomalies.

Submission History Analysis

Compares current submissions against a student's past work (if available) to detect significant deviations in style or quality.

Customizable Sensitivity Levels

Adjust detection thresholds for AI and to suit different assignment types and institutional policies.
